Sikkim - The Land of Beauty

Embarking on a journey from Bangladesh to the captivating city of Gangtok in Sikkim is a venture into the heart of the Eastern Himalayan Range. Nestled at 1437 meters in the Shivalik Hills, Gangtok's natural beauty is indescribable, offering a haven at the high peaks of the Himalayan Mountains. Boasting a population of 30,000, this enchanting city is a must-visit for travel enthusiasts. To immerse yourself in the breathtaking Cloud House of Sikkim, encircle Gangtok.

Journey to Tranquil Beauty - Volaganj Shada Pathor

Madhobpur Lake in Moulovibazar and Volaganj Shada Pathor in Sylhet, Bangladesh. Although the trip began on a subdued note due to the previous night's festivities, the two-hour drive unfolded to reveal a destination near the India-Bangladesh border. Here, majestic Indian mountains stood proudly, casting an extraordinary yet unattainable allure. Volaganj, known for its stone quarries, has witnessed substantial extraction over the years. While many quarries lie abandoned, local miners persist in extracting stones in modest quantities.

Sundarban - The Home Of Royal Bengal

Discover the Sundarbans, a UNESCO World Heritage marvel, with Travel Mate's expertly curated tour. Our early-morning start from Khulna immerses you in the Bay of Bengal's beauty, offering encounters with Royal Bengal Tigers and diverse wildlife. Stay securely on a dedicated boat, ensuring both safety and comfort. Our seasoned guides lead you through lush creeks and canals, offering an affordable and flexible family adventure, fulfilling your desire to explore the wonders of this unique mangrove forest.
